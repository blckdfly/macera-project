Here's a list of essentials and travel tips to help you out
1. Take the path less travelled
  • Passport/ID
  • VISA
  • Boarding Passes
  • Travel Insurance Documents
  • Itinerary and hotel reservations
2. Money and payment methods
  • Sufficient cash
  • VISA
  • Credit/debit cards
  • Traveler's check
3. Health and safety
  • Prescriptions medications
  • Insect repellent
  • Basic first-aid kit
  • Sunscreen
  • FaceMask
  • Travel insurance with medical coverage
4. Electronics and gadgets
  • Mobile phone and charger
  • Universal power adapter/converter
  • Camera or smartphone for capturing memories
  • Power bank
5. Clothing and accesories
  • Weather appropriate clothing
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Swimwear (if applicable)
  • Hat/cap for sun protection
  • Umbrella or raincoat
  • Sunglasses
6. Miscellaneous
  • Snack and water bottle
  • Travel pillow and earplugs for better sleep during flights or long journey
  • Reusable shopping bag
  • Ziplock bags
  • Travel laundry detergent (for long trips)
