About This Area
Labuan Bajo
Labuan Bajo, located on the western tip of Flores Island, Indonesia, is the gateway to the stunning Komodo National Park, home to the world-famous Komodo dragons. This charming fishing town is surrounded by crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and dramatic landscapes, making it a paradise for divers, snorkelers, and adventure seekers. Visitors can explore nearby islands, such as Padar and Rinca, hike to breathtaking viewpoints, and relax on pristine beaches. Labuan Bajo also offers a variety of accommodation and dining options, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay for every traveler.
